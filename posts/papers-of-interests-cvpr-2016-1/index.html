MATCHING

  • FANNG: Fast Approximate Nearest Neighbour Graphs Ben Harwood (CVPR-16) -- Find best match descriptor in a dataset. Directed graph, one node for each descriptor. "Occlusion" property for ideal graph to make sure the search always goes downhill (getting closer to the target) starting at an arbitrary node. Speed up with approximate graph construction and back-track search.

  • Robust Visual Place Recognition With Graph Kernels. Elena Stumm (CVPR-16) ◄◄

  • Needle-Match: Reliable Patch Matching Under High Uncertainty. Or Lotan (CVPR-16) -- Multi-scale jet-style descriptor using pixel intensity directly. Same patch size at all levels. Good matching under lots of noise, blur, deformation. Not scale invariant. ◄
